About Me

I am a Computer Science Graduate from India, currently pursuing my master’s degree in Data Science. My Master's in Data Science from Northeastern University has equipped me with a solid foundation in data analytics, machine learning, and statistical techniques. During my current role as a Research Associate at SATH Lab, I have been actively involved in data collection, manipulation, and analysis, collaborating with various stakeholders to inform policy decisions based on real-time socioeconomic and internet connectivity data. This experience has honed my skills in analyzing complex datasets and deriving actionable insights. In my previous position as a Data Scientist at Cerebral, I successfully developed and implemented CMD-1, achieving over 95% accuracy in identifying crisis messages from electronic medical records (EMR). I have experience in using Python (including pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch), SQL, and other data science tools, aligning with the technical requirements outlined in the job description. As a Data Science intern at CityZenith, Chicago, IL, I developed my interest towards the in-depth data engineering and data handling methods on how well simple findings and visualizations in the data, prediction models based on complicated building data can impact the company’s growth and business, and as a Data Science intern at Innominds inc. I enjoyed building e-commerce models to target users based on their needs and I am fascinated by the power of computation in leveraging data, particularly in the context of Business Intelligence and Strategy-making Analyses. I hope to incorporate my knowledge in Descriptive Analysis of the past data into Predictive Analysis of the future data to strengthen quick and accurate decision-support systems in making critical decisions in hours of need, which is dire for any organization to reach its full potential. Through my collegiate career, I am aspiring to gain qualified knowledge and experience within this field and to eventually undertake any job opportunity.
